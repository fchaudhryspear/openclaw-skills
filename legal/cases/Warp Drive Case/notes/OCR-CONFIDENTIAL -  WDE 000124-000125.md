Information for Former Spearhead Employees and Contractors
All former employees and contractors of Spearhead or a 
Spearhead affiliate, including but not limited to Spearhead 
Group, Spearhead Consulting, FiberWave, Spearhead Networks, 
Spearhead Networks Tech, NextGenTek, Spearhead Technology, 
Spearhead Mobility, and any related entity (collectively, 
“Spearhead”), must complete a verification process if they wish 
to work in AT&T’s Alliance channel, ACC Business channel, or 
APEX channel (collectively, “Alliance Channel”), whether at a 
different Solution Provider, Tech Service Distributor (“TSD”), or 
sub-agent, or by setting up their own Solution Provider, TSD, or 
sub-agent.  This requirement applies regardless of when the 
individual worked for Spearhead and regardless of whether they 
already work elsewhere in the Alliance Channel.  
You can begin this process by filling out the questionnaire on 
this website.  Please click on Submit Application in the upper 
right corner of your screen to find the questionnaire.  We 
encourage you to provide responses to the questionnaire to the
