PARTNER AGREEMENT 
This Partner Agreement (the “Agreement”) is made and 
entered into as of the Effective Date set forth below by and between 
SPEARHEAD NETWORKS TECH, INC., an Texas corporation 
(“SPEARHEAD”), and the partner listed below (“Partner”) (each one 
separately known as a “Party” and jointly as the “Parties”). 
Whereas, SPEARHEAD has agreements with the 
communications and information technology providers (“Suppliers”) 
listed in Exhibit B to market their respective products and services 
(the “Services”) to end user customers (“Customers”); and 
Whereas, the Parties have agreed that Partner may solicit 
orders (“Orders”) for the Services as an independent sales 
representative of SPEARHEAD. 
Now therefore, in consideration of the mutual promises 
contained herein and for other good and valuable consideration, the 
receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, the Parties 
hereby agree as follows: 
1. AGREEMENT. SPEARHEAD hereby appoints Partner as its
non-exclusive sales representative to promote and market the
Services, and Partner hereby accepts such appointment. Partner shall
identify, cultivate and generate Orders for Services from prospective
Customers on SPEARHEAD’s behalf.  Partner shall abide by
SPEARHEAD’s and each applicable Supplier’s policies and
procedures. Partner understands that a Supplier may, in its sole
discretion, reject any prospective customers and/or Orders submitted
by Partner and SPEARHEAD shall have no liability to Partner for
such rejection..
2. TERM.
2.1 The initial term (the “Initial Term”) of this Agreement shall 
commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of one (1) 
year, and shall automatically renew thereafter for successive one (1) 
year terms (each a “Renewal Term”) if not terminated by either Party 
by providing at least thirty (30) days prior written notice. The Initial 
Term and any applicable Renewal Terms shall hereafter be referred 
to collectively as the “Term”.  This Agreement may be terminated: 
(a) by either Party in the event of a breach of any term or condition
hereof by the other Party that is not cured within thirty (30) days after
receipt by the breaching party of written notice of such breach; (b) by
either Party for convenience on thirty (30) days prior written notice;
(c) by mutual written consent of the Parties hereto; (d) by
SPEARHEAD immediately if Partner submits any Orders that are
fraudulent, are procured through misrepresentation or lack proper
Customer authorization; (e) by SPEARHEAD immediately if Partner
breaches the non-solicitation or confidentiality covenants hereof; (g)
by SPEARHEAD upon the assignment of this Agreement by Partner
without SPEARHEAD’s prior written consent, which consent will
not be unreasonably withheld.
2.2  If this Agreement is terminated by SPEARHEAD for any 
reason, SPEARHEAD shall continue to pay Commissions on all 
products and services sold to Customers referred to 
SPEARHEAD by Partner; including but not limited to upgrades, 
contract extensions, and new products and services. 
3. COMMISSIONS.
3.1 SPEARHEAD shall compensate Partner hereunder in the form of 
commissions (“Commissions”) on the Services sold by Partner.  
Partner’s Commissions shall be calculated as a percentage (the “Pass-
Through Percentage”) of the compensation actually paid to 
SPEARHEAD by each applicable Supplier. Subject to the other terms 
and conditions set forth herein, the Pass-Through Percentage is set 
forth in Exhibit A attached hereto and incorporated herein.  
Commissions shall only be available on Services provided pursuant 
to Orders that have been delivered by Partner and approved by the 
Supplier. An Order shall be valid only if signed by the Customer and 
accepted by the Supplier.  The Parties agree to the following: (i) 
SPEARHEAD may receive payments from Suppliers as bonuses 
(“Bonuses”) in recognition of SPEARHEAD overall performance, 
and (ii) Bonuses attributed to Customers referred by Parter will 
generate Commissions to Partner at the current Commission rate. 
3.2 In addition to the requirements of Section 3.1, Partner shall only 
be paid (and Commissions for a Customer shall only be deemed 
earned) when the Supplier has actually paid SPEARHEAD the 
commissions owed to SPEARHEAD for the Customer.  Where less 
than the full commission is paid to SPEARHEAD by a Supplier, the 
Partner shall only be entitled to its Pass-Through Percentage of such 
amount actually paid.  Partner acknowledges and agrees that it shall 
be subject to a changed Commission payment, no Commission 
payment, reduced Commission payment or delayed Commission 
payment if, for any reason, SPEARHEAD: (a) does not receive 
payment from a Supplier; (b) receives a reduced payment from a 
Supplier; or (c) receives a delayed payment from a Supplier.   
3.3 Commissions shall be paid by SPEARHEAD to the Partner 
within 30 (thirty) days of the close of the calendar month in which 
SPEARHEAD receives payment from the applicable Supplier(s) 
along with the information necessary from the Supplier for 
SPEARHEAD to calculate Partner's Commission.
